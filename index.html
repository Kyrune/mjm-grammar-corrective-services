Info

- How It Works -

Step 1

When you email me, please include:
- Your work's word count.
- What it is you are writing, such as genre, subject, goal, desired outcomes, etc.
- Any questions you may have.
- If you wish, also share a little about yourself. Knowing something about you will help me to better understand your writing.

- If your work is a book (about 10,000 words or more), please include the first chapter with your email. For anything below that, I will use your work's word count to help you decide what to send me.
I will use the first chapter to assess your work. I will also edit, for free, the first three pages, unless the work's word count is less than 10,000. Such cases will be determined on an individual basis. This is also an opportunity for you to decide if you like my work.

Step 2

I will email you with an editing service recommendation and a price. Please see the Services section for general pricing information, and please note that they are estimates. Once you have agreed to an arrangement, I will email a contract for you to sign. This contract has been designed to clarify the expectations of both parties, so please read it thoroughly. Never fear, I've kept it as clear and concise as possible. If you have any questions or concerns regarding the contract, it can be adjusted, if needed, before it is signed. The goal of our contract is to protect you as much as me.

Step 3

Payments are made through PayPal in sectional deposits. For example, an initial deposit based on 50% of your work's word count must be paid before I begin the editing process. If you prefer, this deposit percentage can be reduced into thirds, or even fourths depending on the size of your work. The contract also includes a clause for cancellation. If you or I needed to cancel for whatever reason, I would refund you the amount equal to the word count of your work that has not been edited and email you what I have completed. If no payment is received at any point, I will withhold the edited work. The contract will further explain this process.

Please Note

I reserve the right to turn down your work if it is illegible or requires an editing process more extensive than what I offer. I would be happy to give you advice on how to improve your writing skills so you may return at a later date. If I edited your work before it was ready, it would result in a waste of your valuable time and money.

I reserve the right to refuse to edit any illicit or overly sexual content. Romance is one thing, porn is another, and I decide when that line is crossed. If you are uncertain about a particular scene, please share it and I will let you know whether or not I am comfortable with it.

I reserve the right to stop editing at any time for any reason. Whatever I have edited up until that point will be emailed to you and I will refund the amount equal to the amount I have not edited, based on word count.
